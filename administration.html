    We also have the UG Compssa or the University of Ghana Computer Science Students Association: Is a student-led organization that focuses on promoting and supporting computer science education and related activities at the University of Ghana. They organize various events, workshops, and initiatives to enhance the learning experience and foster a sense of community among computer science students. If you are interested in computer science, the UG Compssa can be a fantastic resource for you!
