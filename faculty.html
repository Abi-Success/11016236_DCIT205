    GET TO KNOW US

    Our Community

    If you are excited about tackling a significant challenge, you’re in the perfect spot! The Computer Science at the University of Ghana motivates individual to be dynamic and courageous when confronting fresh obstacles that go beyond what we can imagine.

    Our Organization

    The University of Ghana is one of the prestigious public universities in Africa. Its influence extends beyond individuals and our region, making a profound impact on the world. UG is dedicated to transforming ideas into real-world impact. With over 38,000 students enrolled each year, including approximately 2.64% in computer science. To learn more about our impact, you can visit our news site, UG News.

    Building Our Future

    So wat sets apart our students, faculty, and community members? Most essentially its our unwavering optimism. It’s the strong connection we share with others, both locally and internationally. We firmly believe that by working together, we can make a positive impact on the world.
