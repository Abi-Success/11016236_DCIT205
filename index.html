    WELCOME TO UNIVERSITY OF GHANA, COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

    COURSES WE OFFER

    Undergraduate Programmes

    The department offer two undegraduate degree programs: Bachelor of Science as well as Bachelor in Information Technology. To be eligible for admission into the computer science programs at level 200 Level 200, candiddates must have complete certain courses at Level 100, including Algebra and Trigonometry, Introduction to Computer Science I, and Introduction to Computer Science II. Additionally every student in the Department of Computer Science is required to have a laptop of their own with a minimum specification of an i3 64-bit Processor, 8GB RAM, and a 500GB hard drive or 256GB solid-state drive.

    MPhil and Msc Programmes:

    The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana offers two post graduate programs: a Master of Science (MSc.) in Computer Science and a Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) in Computer Science.
    The MSc. program lasts for one year and can be taken on weekends or evenings. On the other hand the MPhil. is a two year program. The first year consists of taught courses, while the second year focuses on research and disseration. Students in these programs gain a deep understanding of theoretical computer science and develop advanced technical skills to deal with complex global programs using technology as a powerful tool.

    Ph.D.

    The doctoral program at our university is a full-time research program that typically takes around 4 years to complete. It requires a high level of commitment from students. Students are expected to live near the university and fully engage in their studies.lond side coursework, the department organizes regular seminars where students and faculty discuss and analyze research in the field. The department's research areas include Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics,Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cyber Security.
