    ADMISSION

    Greetings,
    The University of Ghana attracts students from various regions in Ghana, across Africa, and around the Globe, bringing with them a rich tapestry of perspectives, experiences, and cultural backgrounds. The students financial Aid Office (SFAO) is dedicated to supporting financially constrained Ghanaian undergraduate applicants. Additionally, the University actively promotes the involvement of International students through direct enrollment and exchange programs, with a current enrollment of nearly 1,500 students representing over 71 countries.
